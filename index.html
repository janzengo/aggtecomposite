Why Choose Us

All of our products are designed to be simple to install. Nothing available on our site requires specialist tools and the light-weight materials speed up the assembly process. We’ll have your exterior looking stylish in no time. At AGGTE WPC Cladding & Decking, we believe in minimising wastage and encouraging sustainability. Our AGGTE calculator can help you work out exactly how much material you will need to complete your project.


Get in touch with one of our team members for more information on any products across our site. Alternatively, for more information on assembling your composite deck, download our installation guide. Our wood plastic composite materials are all engineered to be more durable than wood. The additives strengthen the existing hardwood properties which means your deck, cladding or fencing is tougher than ordinary products. Our composite materials do not absorb water like wooden alternatives which means they are able to effectively endure weather conditions and are easier to keep clean.
